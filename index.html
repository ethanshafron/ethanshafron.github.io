PhD Candidate, Systems Ecology · University of Montana
Scientific Research Affiliate · NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory

I use airborne and satellite imaging spectroscopy to characterize plant functional traits and ecosystem processes at landscape to continental scales. My dissertation work integrates hyperspectral remote sensing, field ecology, and Bayesian modeling to study forest carbon dynamics and photosynthesis-growth coupling.

I'm a member of the Global Climate and Ecology Lab at the University of Montana under Dr. Ashley Ballantyne.
